CURRENT PROJECT

2026 · Work in progress

Dish Forecasting with Set-Based Neural Networks

Predicting the demand for one dish requires understanding the other dishes offered at the same time. The menu is therefore naturally modeled as a set rather than a fixed vector or an independent time series.

Problem

Estimate dish-level choice rates for changing menus across multiple restaurant sites and dates.

Approach

Compare simple baselines with permutation-invariant architectures, including DeepSets and attention-based set encoders.

Evaluation

Analyze MAE, RMSE, WAPE, and calibration at dish, menu, date, and restaurant levels.
